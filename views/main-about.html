About

Damusiq.com is a social web application for music enthusiasts. Damusiq offers a full library of free music scores, all of them non-licensed or under the Creative Commons licenes.

Damusiq has also a set of useful tools for music students in order to perform advanced searches and to explore the content in different ways, such as by instrument, historical period, and special categories.

Damusiq provides also a revolucionary way to understand the music history through timelines. We offer for each specific composer's category, a complete timeline with useful information in context.

Damusiq is growing faster, we are improving day by day, and we hope that Damusiq will be your main choice teh next time you find for music scores.
